Utilites

.group
➜ Includes the clearfix mixin.

.hidden
➜ Hides the element.

.left
➜ Floats the element to the left.

.right
➜ Floats the element to the right.

.disabled
➜ Disables the element. Also applies to the disabled attribute.

readonly (attribute)
➜ Applies read-only styles to the element.

.antialiased
➜ Applies font smoothing to help the readability of light text on a dark background.

.rounded
➜ Applies a border radius to the element.

Colors

.primary
➜ Applies the primary color as a background to the element and assigns the text color to white.

.secondary
➜ Applies the secondary color as a background to the element and assigns the text color to white.
